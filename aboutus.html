Founders Story

All began during the lockdown when we participated in a B-Plan Competition
which we could not win but this idea had won our hearts.
To back this up, we could see many queries regarding proper laptop selection from our peers on our facebook groups. Determined, we set up on this journey to make one's laptop selection process easier. With the vision that our product will help someone to buy a laptop quickly and more reliably gives us the motivation to continue.
